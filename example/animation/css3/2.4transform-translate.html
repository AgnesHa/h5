位移：translate

translateX(a):水平方向移动a

	.bg1{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translateX(0);}
    .bg1:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translateX(100px);}

translateY(a): 垂直方向移动a

.bg2{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translateY(0);}
.bg2:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translateY(-50px);}

translateZ(a): 垂直Z轴（这个Z轴是一条垂直于X和Y所在平面的一条线）移动

.bg3{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 100px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translateZ(0);}
.bg3:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translateZ(200px);}

scale(a,a)等同于translateZ(a):水平方向缩放a倍,垂直方向缩放a倍

.bg33{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scale(1,1);}
.bg33:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scale(1.5,1.5);}

translate(a,b): 水平方向移动a,垂直方向移动b

.bg4{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translate(0,0);}
.bg4:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translate(100px,100px);}

translate3d(x,y,z):水平方向移动x,垂直方向移动y,垂直z轴方向移动z

.bg5{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translate3d(0,0,0);}
.bg5:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) translate3d(100px,100px,100px);}
