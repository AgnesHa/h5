缩放：scale

scaleX(a):水平方向缩放a倍

.bg1{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scaleX(1);}
.bg1:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scaleX(1.5);}

scaleY(a):垂直方向缩放a倍

.bg2{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scaleY(1);}
.bg2:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scaleY(1.5);}

scale(a,b):水平方向缩放a倍,垂直方向缩放b倍

.bg3{width:200px;height:200px;background:green;margin:0 auto; margin-top: 150px;transition:1s;-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scale(1,1);}
.bg3:hover{-webkit-transform:perspective(600px) scale(1.5,1.5);}
